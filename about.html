Welcome to My World!

I’m a Mechanical Engineering student at the California Institute of Technology (CalTech) with a passion for creating things that make a difference. Originally from Baton Rouge, Louisiana, I later moved to Fairfax, Virginia, and now study in Pasadena, California, to pursue my higher education.


Growing up, I've always had a broad range of interests—whether it was tinkering with gadgets, writing poetry, or painting. The common thread? My desire to create.


When I'm not designing or building something, you can find me spending time with family and friends, exploring new places, or enjoying a good movie.
